Crystal Collectors

Collectors Guide
  • You will be given a magic number at the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your crystal count.
  • You win the game by matching your crystal count to the magic number. You lose the game if your crystal count goes above the magic number.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
